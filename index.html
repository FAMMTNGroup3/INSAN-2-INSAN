INSAN 2 INSAN is a non-profit website were people IN-NEED can ask for the help anonymously, and organizations can get to them and give the help needed.


By registering your non-profit organization in INSAN 2 INSAN website we will help you to reach to the right people to provide your services to them. We believe in the importance of the NGOs, INGOs and the local organizations in making a diffrance in the community were INSAN 2 INSAN came to connect them together.
You can also Donate and make a positive impact to top crisis and issues happening around the world. as we believe each one of us can make a diffrance with their small contribution.

Non-governmental organization (NGO)


NGOs are a subgroup of organizations founded by citizens, which include clubs and associations that provide services to their members and others. NGOs are usually nonprofit organizations, and many of them are active in humanitarianism or the social sciences. Surveys indicate that NGOs have a high degree of public trust, which can make them a useful proxy for the concerns of society and stakeholders.
